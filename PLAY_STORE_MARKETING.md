# Play Store name & marketing guide (Applooms · com.applooms.aab2apk)

Use this as an expert marketer’s checklist to get found on the Play Store and convert visitors into installs.

---

## 1. App name suggestions (by Applooms)

Pick **one** for the **Store listing title** (30 characters max):

| Option | Title | Why |
|--------|--------|-----|
| **A** | **AAB2APK – Convert & Analyse** | Clear, matches your logo “AAB2APK ANALYSE”, good for search. |
| **B** | **AAB to APK Converter – Applooms** | Very clear for “aab to apk” searches; “Applooms” = brand. |
| **C** | **Applooms AAB2APK Analyse** | Puts brand first; “Analyse” matches logo and analysis feature. |

**Recommendation:** Use **A** or **B** so “AAB” and “APK” appear early (Play uses the first words for relevance).

---

## 2. Short description (80 characters max)

Use every character for search and benefit:

**Option 1 (keyword‑heavy):**  
`Convert AAB to APK, analyse app bundle & permissions. Install APK in one tap. By Applooms`

**Option 2 (benefit‑first):**  
`Turn Android App Bundles into APKs. Analyse AAB/APK, view permissions, install. Applooms`

**Option 3 (minimal):**  
`AAB to APK converter & analyser. View permissions, install APK. By Applooms`

Include: **AAB**, **APK**, **convert**, **analyse**, **permissions**, **install**, **Applooms**.

---

## 3. Full description (first 2–3 lines matter most)

Play uses the start of the description for search and “read more” preview. Put the main offer and keywords in the first 2–3 lines.

**Example opening:**

```
AAB2APK by Applooms converts Android App Bundles (.aab) to universal APKs and analyses both AAB and APK files.

• Convert AAB to APK in one tap
• Analyse any AAB or APK: size, permissions, manifest
• View full list of permissions with clear labels
• Install the generated APK with the system installer
• Share APK after conversion

Built for developers and testers who need a quick way to get an APK from an app bundle or inspect permissions and content of AAB/APK files.
```

Then add 1–2 short paragraphs about use cases (e.g. testing, sharing builds, checking permissions) and end with “From Applooms” or “Applooms – developer tools”.

---

## 4. Play Store search (how people find you)

People search for things like:

- **aab to apk**
- **convert aab to apk**
- **aab converter**
- **apk from aab**
- **analyse aab**
- **app bundle to apk**

Your **title** and **short description** are the main signals. Make sure they contain:

- “AAB” and “APK”
- “convert” or “converter”
- “analyse” or “analyze”
- “permissions” (optional but helpful)

Avoid stuffing; keep sentences natural.
